# Origin of the Name — Sage Kaizen

## Why “Sage”?

The system was designed to behave not as a reactive chatbot, but as a:

- Thoughtful architect
- Teacher
- Research companion
- System designer

“Sage” reflects wisdom, deliberation, and long-context reasoning — embodied by the Architect Brain running on high-performance GPUs.

It represents clarity over speed, depth over impulse, and guidance over reaction.

---

## Why “Kaizen”?

Kaizen is a Japanese philosophy meaning **continuous improvement**.

The project follows Kaizen principles through:

- Iterative GPU tuning
- Prompt evolution
- Measured benchmarking
- RAG refinements
- Modular architectural upgrades
- Documented design decisions (ADR discipline)

The system is never “finished.”
It is always improving.

---

## Combined Meaning

Sage Kaizen reflects:

Wisdom guided by discipline.  
Intelligence refined through iteration.  
Architecture strengthened through continuous improvement.

It is both a system and a philosophy.
