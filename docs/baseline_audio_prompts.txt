🧠 Pro Tips (Based on Your Architecture)
1. Always combine pipelines

Best results come from:

Audio → CLAP embedding
      → (optional) Whisper transcript
      → Jina CLIP text embedding
      → RAG retrieval
2. Use routing (CRITICAL)
if speech:
    → Whisper → text pipeline
else:
    → CLAP → audio pipeline
3. Hybrid answers are strongest

Instead of:

“It sounds like rain”

Do:

“It sounds like rain. I found similar recordings in your database and images of nighttime rainfall environments.”
